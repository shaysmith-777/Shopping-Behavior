## Answering Questions
1. How does purchase amount vary across different age groups?
The results demonstrates that purchase amounts vary across different age groups indicating meaningful differences in consumer spending behavior. Certain age groups appear to contribute a higher average transaction value, which may reflect stronger purchasing power or increased engagement with fashion products.

2. Do customers spend more or less for transaction?
The results indicate that customers who utilized promo codes spent slightly less on average per transaction than customers who did not use promotional discounts. This suggests that promotional offers may primarily attract price sensitive consumers rather than heavily increasing overall spending. FlashFash may benefit from implementing promotions that encourage higher transaction values.

3. Which season generate the highest total revenue?
The analysis reveals noticeable differences in total revenue across seasons with Winter having the highest revenue overall. Increased revenue during specific seasons may be influenced by factors such as holidays, climate related shopping trends and seasonal fashion demand.

4. Which states have the highest average purchase value?
The data findings show that the average purchase values across states are pretty consistent with only minor variations between the states. While certain states demonstrate slightly higher average transaction amounts the differences are not enough to suggest significant differences in spending behavior.
